How to download extensions

  • Step 1:

    Click Download Extension Button

  • Step 2:

    Navigate to the extensions page for your browser. chrome://extensions for Google Chrome and edge://extensions for Microsoft Edge

  • Step 3:

    Turn on developer mode

  • Step: 4

    Move zipped files to a folder or unzip the files

  • Step 5:

    Press "Load Unpacked" button

  • Step 6:

    Navigate to the folder where you saved the files

  • Step 7:

    Press select folder

  • Step 8:

    Enjoy new extension
